## Overview

MathDown is designed with privacy in mind. We believe your documents and data belong to you.

## Data Collection

**MathDown does not collect any personal data.**

The app operates entirely on your device. Your documents, calculations, and settings are stored locally and are never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

## What We Don't Collect

- No analytics or usage tracking
- No document content or metadata
- No personal information
- No device identifiers
- No location data
- No cookies or tracking pixels

## Local Storage

Your documents stay on your device. We never see them.

## iCloud

If you choose to store documents in iCloud Drive, that storage is managed by Apple according to their [Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). MathDown does not have access to your iCloud account or credentials.

## Third-Party Services

MathDown does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

## Network Access

MathDown may access the network only to:

- Load images from URLs you explicitly include in your documents

No other network requests are made by the application.

## Children's Privacy

MathDown does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page and the "last updated" date below.
